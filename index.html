About Tatkalorry

Tatkalorry is a tech-enabled logistics platform focused on transforming the transportation sector within the Indian Ceramic Industry, especially in Morbi. It serves as a vital link connecting the transportation fraternity with manufacturers and dealers, aiming for efficient, timely, and cost-effective delivery of goods. The platform provides solutions for both part-load and full-load deliveries, even offering door-to-door service with competitive quotes.

Addressing Industry Challenges & The Solution

Common Logistical Hurdles:

  • Consignment delivery limitations
  • Irregular dispatching from manufacturing units
  • Accumulation of unused stock at buyers' ends
  • Lack of sampling opportunities due to unaffordable logistics costs
  • Consignment booking hassles
  • Challenges with Intercity & Intra-city Transport

Our Innovative Solution:

A team of veterans from the ceramic industry developed **TATKALORRY**, a mobile application designed to bridge these gaps. It connects manufacturers, dealers, truck drivers, truck owners, and transporters for flexible and efficient ceramic goods deliveries.

We empower businesses by streamlining operations, reducing costs, and ensuring seamless movement of goods across Pan Gujarat.

How It Works

Getting started with Tatkalorry is straightforward. Follow these simple steps to streamline your logistics.

Place an Order

Users can place orders by calling our dedicated line: +91 9974074074.

Rapid Processing

Orders for samples, full deliveries, or part deliveries are processed efficiently within **24 hours**.

Sign Up

Download the Tatkalorry app (available on Android & iOS) or connect with our customer care.

Smart Matching

Users input load or truck information. The platform automatically forwards this to other members based on load type, location, destination, and availability.

Instant Notifications

Users receive timely notifications via SMS, call, or email as soon as a suitable match is found.

Pricing, Payment & Pick-ups

Pricing

Calculated based on the **exact or volumetric weight** (whichever is higher) and the **destination**.

Payment

Currently, payment is **cash-on-delivery**, ensuring a simple and direct transaction.

Pick-ups

Can be scheduled **immediately** (within 60 minutes for Morbi to Morbi service) or for a **future date** as per your convenience.

Address Changes

Please note: **Pick-up addresses cannot be changed once scheduled**; a new booking is required for any modifications.
